Final Project Proposal

Topic

I will be making a homepage website for an imaginary cafe I'd probably open or like to see come to life. It'll be under the assumption of an imaginary brand called "it's Peachy" selling drinks. I also hope to keep the "in-ideation" appeal of this website through intentionally including rough sketches.

Audience

Anyone and everyone interested in a cute cafe spot or my rough ideation of a cafe. It will be a customer interested in visiting and buying something from my cafe.

Design & Style

I hope to give the website a cute, natural, and modern appearance with peachy pink accents, greenery, and warm whites/sunrays and a bit of orange/peachy tones. An emphasis on sleek, modern, high-end, nature-loving, fruit-loving, aesthetic, and relaxing atmosphere will be the goal of the design.
