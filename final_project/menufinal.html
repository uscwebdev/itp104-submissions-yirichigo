Beverages

Matcha Latte w/ Azuki (Ice/Hot)

Crafted with care, our Matcha Latte is a velvety blend of premium matcha powder and silky steamed milk, resulting in a rich and comforting beverage that captivates the senses. Immerse yourself in the smooth and slightly sweet notes of red beans, perfectly complementing the earthy undertones of the matcha. Each sip is a dance of textures, as the creamy latte meets the tender, sweet red beans for a truly enchanting taste sensation.

Strawberry Milk

Immerse yourself in a symphony of flavors as velvety milk meets the luscious essence of fresh, ripe strawberries. Treat yourself to the simple joy of this timeless concoction and let the vibrant flavors transport you to a blissful moment of pure indulgence.

Coffee Ice Milk Latte (with Coffee Ice or Jelly) (Ice/Hot)

Watch as your latte takes on a mesmerizing transformation, with each sip delivering the perfect balance of bold coffee flavor and a smooth, refreshing chill. Our coffee ice cubes are a revelation, preserving the integrity of your drink without dilution, ensuring that every moment of enjoyment is as invigorating as the first.

Einspänner Latte (Espresso/Matcha/Chocolate and Ice/Hot)

Originating from the heart of Europe, this decadent creation is a beloved classic, expertly crafted to satisfy both coffee aficionados and dessert enthusiasts alike. The aromatic elixir's foundation is its freshly brewed espresso. This espresso is topped with a generous dollop of fresh, cool whipped cream and a sprinkle of cocoa powder.

Fresh Peach Tea (Green/Black/Oolong) (Ice/Hot)

It's Peachy's Fresh Peach Tea is a refreshing symphony of quality tea leaves, gently steeped to perfection, and generously adorned with slices of juicy, fresh peaches. Delight in the bright, sun-kissed flavors. Whether you're seeking a revitalizing iced tea for a warm afternoon or a comforting hot tea to unwind, our Fresh Peach Tea is the epitome of summer in a cup. Served over ice and garnished with a sprig of mint, it's not just a drink; it's a celebration of nature's bounty, carefully curated for your enjoyment.

Savory

Carbonara Pasta

Our Carbonara Pasta is a masterpiece of simplicity and sophistication. Picture strands of pasta generously coated in a creamy amalgamation of eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, and Parmesan, creating a luscious sauce that clings to each noodle. The dish is adorned with crispy pancetta, infusing every bite with a delightful crunch and a symphony of flavors.

Marinara Pasta

Crafted with care and culinary passion, our marinara sauce is a symphony of ripe tomatoes, garlic, onions, and aromatic herbs, slow-simmered to perfection. Each twirl of al dente pasta is generously coated in this robust and savory sauce, delivering a taste of timeless Italian tradition with every forkful.
