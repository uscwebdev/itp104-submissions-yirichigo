Our Story

The story of It's Peachy began with Yirena, a passionate foodie and fruit enthusiast, who dreamt of creating a space where people could escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Drawing inspiration from the vibrant and nurturing qualities of peaches, Yirena envisioned a haven where patrons could immerse themselves in an atmosphere of relaxation, happiness, and holistic well-being. The café was designed to be a sanctuary for the senses, with soft pastel hues, comfortable furniture, and lush greenery that transported visitors to a world of serenity. Upon entering It's Peachy, the aroma of fresh fruit teas envelop guests, instantly lifting their spirits and inviting them to embark on a journey of self-discovery and nourishment. It's Peachy became renowned for its signature fresh fruit teas, each concoction carefully crafted to not only tantalize taste buds but also to provide a boost of natural energy and essential nutrients. From Peachy Hibiscus Harmony to Mango Mint Serenity, every sip is a celebration and a moment of self-treat. The café's menu was a symphony of flavors, featuring a selection of wholesome snacks and treats made from locally sourced, organic ingredients. The emphasis on nutritious offerings aligns with It's Peachy's commitment to promoting a balanced and nourishing lifestyle. Patrons could enjoy guilt-free indulgence, knowing that every bite was a step towards self-care and well-being.
